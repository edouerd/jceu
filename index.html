Save the date

Jenny and Edouard will be celebrating their 8th year anniversary with a wedding ceremony in the Garment District of New York, New York, on September 9th, 2017.

From Phoenix, Arizona, Jenny attended Arizona State University to pursue design. She is currently a Designer in New York City at an international architecture firm. She met Edouard when she needed a to use someone's printer and a friend asked, "Have you met Eddy?" One of her favorite memories was getting drawings from him left on her door.

From Tucson, Arizona, Edouard studied Industrial Design at Arizona State University. After graduation he moved to Austin, then New York City as a Product Designer. He remembers seeing Jenny running around in the hallways on campus. One of his favorite memories was riding his bike around the city with her sitting on the handlebars.
